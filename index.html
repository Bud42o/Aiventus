Mailing Bags

Our eco-friendly Mailing Bags are designed specifically for shipping goods across e-commerce, retail and logistics operations. Produced from recycled LDPE film, these mailers provide a durable, water-resistant and are perfect shipping solution.

Available in various sizes, thicknesses, and print options, the bags can be tailored to specific business requirements. Configurations such as single or double seal strips with perforation provide reliable closure and support convenient return options, while optional handle features are available upon request. As a practical and sustainable packaging option, they ensure secure closure and protection from dispatch to delivery.

Stretch Bands

The Stretch Band is a versatile pallet packaging solution designed to securely hold protection corners in place without damaging the primary packaging. It removes easily, leaving no marks and preserving the integrity and appearance of cartons and boxes.

Available in multiple widths and lengths, the Stretch Band can be tailored to specific packaging dimensions and pallet configurations. As a complementary packaging accessory, it enhances load stability and keeps products securely positioned during transportation and storage.
